Paris Keswani has been quietly and humbly serving those in need not just in her community but on a global scale with an unshakeable commitment to the preservation of human rights and equality before the eyes of the law for all. Paris has entrenched herself into the world of advocacy for the homeless, displaced children, orphans, abused mothers, and people displaced by catastrophic natural events.

Over the years her relentless advocacy and unflinching dedication to improving the well being of others endeared her to many friends and admirers from civil society and helped form alliances and progressive partnerships with her New York Non-Profit Organization, Great Giving Charitable Global Foundation, Inc.

Born In India , Paris relocated to the United States, where she attained Honors In Public Administration while preserving her love for gemology. She later became a certified gemologist and successful entrepreneur initially working her way through the ropes as a jewelry designer and sales associate. Now as an accomplished jeweler and beauty queen, Paris has irrevocably dedicated her entire professional life to the service of humanity, promotion of global peace, eradication of poverty and provision of housing for all.
