Our History

Great Giving Charitable Global Foundation Inc., was founded as a NewYork State Not-For-Profit organization in June 15, 2020 with a mission to bridge the gap between sadness and happiness in our children.We are a willing catalyst in global movement to positively change the lives of underprivileged children seeking to eliminate poverty and infant mortality amongst other things.

Our Mission

We organized this global humanitarian non-profit organization because we sincerely love all children no matter where they are in the world.We are here to assist with finding available resources and systems that can help underprivileged children in need with health care, food, shelter, education, clean water and inspire them to invigorate their inner potential.

Paris Keswani CEO & Founder of Great Giving Foundation.
